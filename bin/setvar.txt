setvar v0.1.0

Unfortunately, `cmd` doesn't have an easy-to-use,
built-in way of assigning the output of a command
to an environment variable.

`setvar` fixes that:

  Usage:
  
    $ setvar <var_name> <command> [--echo]

  Examples:

    $ setvar me whoami
    $ echo %me%
    c-3po\mark

    $ setvar me whoami --echo
    c-3po\mark
